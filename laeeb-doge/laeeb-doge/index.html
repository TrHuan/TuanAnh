Inspired by the World Cup 2022 Mascot, Laeeb Doge project was founded by a diverse group of individuals with a shared love of digital money and football.

What is?

Ranging from web developers and analysts to graphic artists and talent-marketers. The goal of the Laeeb Doge project is to intergrate the blockchain technology into betting system and help poor-loving football children all around the world.

Bet:

Laeeb Doge Tokens can be used to bet on your favorite team in World Cup. There will be many types of bet in our DApp (Full-time result, Correct Score, First Goal Scorer, Last Goal Scorer...)

CHARITY:

A fixed part of transaction fees will be used for support poor children football passion. Chairity activies and funds will be transparent to Laeeb Doge investors.

DAO VOTE:

Every 7 days, Users are able to suggest and vote for their favorite meme tokens. The Laeeb Doge DAO mechanism will automatically purchase and distribute the token with the most votes as soon as the voting is over based on the Laeeb Doge tokens holding rate.

Only big holders can propose meme tokens on our DAO Dapp.
